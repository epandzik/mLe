Hi!

Who I Am

I grew up in Kansas City, and have travelled throughout the U.S. for school and work. I’ve lived in Milwaukee, Omaha, Portland and even Galway, Ireland for six months. I’ve been to Europe several times and travelled to Japan with a pharmaceutical company.
I’ve learned we are all the same, wishing for happiness and freedom from fear. It’s my intention to continue to learn, to be open to new experiences and to grow in understanding.

Work

I’ve worked in two main fields in my life — research science and design/art. While it might be easy to think they can be chronologically separated, these threads have been intertwined throughout my life. Science opened my mind and invited me to become skeptical.
After leaving biological research, I entered into design. Art was my first love, if not my only one. Having a good eye for what looks “right” has steered me well throughout my life. Balance and symmetry are staples, but breaking those up are the spice of it all.
